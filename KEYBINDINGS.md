# Keybindings

## Global
- `q`: Quit the application.
- `Space`: Toggle between Play and Pause.
- `o`: Decrease Volume.
- `p`: Increase Volume.
- `CTRL+R`: Switch to Radio mode (Online).
- `/`: Enter Search mode.
- `Tab`: Switch between Playlist and Track views.

## Navigation
- `j` or `Down Arrow`: Highlight the next item in the list.
- `k` or `Up Arrow`: Highlight the previous item in the list.
- `l` or `L`: Skip to the Next Track.
- `h` or `H`: Skip to the Previous Track.
- `Enter`: Play the currently highlighted track or radio station.

## Search Mode
- `Characters`: Type to filter the current list.
- `Backspace`: Remove the last character.
- `Esc` or `Enter`: Exit search mode and return to navigation.
- `o/p`: Adjust Volume even while searching.
